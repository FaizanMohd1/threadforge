FAQ
Common questions.
What is the minimum order quantity?
+

Koi minimum order quantity nahi hai. Aap sirf 1 piece bhi order kar sakte hain. Bulk pricing 50+ pieces se start hoti hai — aur 100, 250, aur 500+ units pe aur discount milta hai.

How long does production take?
+

Standard orders 24–48 ghante mein process hote hain. Custom print ya embroidery orders mein 48–72 ghante lagte hain. 500+ pieces ke bulk orders mein 5–7 business days lag sakte hain. Delivery time aapki location par depend karta hai.

Do you offer white label and private label services?
+

Yes. We offer full white label and private label solutions — custom tags, labels, packaging, and branding. Your customers will receive products that look like they came directly from your brand.

What file formats do you accept for printing?
+

We accept PNG (transparent background, min 300 DPI), PDF, AI, and PSD files. For embroidery, we also accept DST and EMB files. Our team will review and prep your artwork before production.

Do you ship all over India?
+

Haan. Hum poore India mein ship karte hain. Delivery time aur charges aapki location ke hisaab se vary karte hain. Dropshipping partners ke liye hum seedha unke customers ko ship karte hain aapke branding ke saath.

What is your return and quality guarantee policy?
+

Every order goes through quality control before shipping. If any item is defective or doesn't match your approved sample, we replace it at no charge. We stand behind every product we make.
